What are the diffrent types of blood transfusion?

The donated blood is divided into different parts and stored in special bags or bottles,
        which can then be given to you.Only the part your body needs. The three main parts include:

        Red blood cells that carry oxygen to tissues and organs.

        Platelets that help stop the bleeding.

        Plasma containing blood-clotting factors (to help stop bleeding), as well as other
        proteins and antibodie counter.
